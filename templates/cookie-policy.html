What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. Similar technologies may store a preference or help a website understand how it is used.

How TrueNotice uses them

We may use strictly necessary storage to remember settings such as your cookie-consent choice and to support site features. If analytics or advertising is enabled, additional cookies may be used for measurement, fraud prevention, personalization, and ad delivery.

Managing consent

Use the controls in the consent message when it appears. You can also clear site data or block cookies in your browser. Blocking necessary storage may affect some features. You can revisit this policy at any time from the footer.

Third-party cookies

Advertising and analytics providers may set their own cookies. Their use is governed by their policies. Where required, TrueNotice should load non-essential advertising or analytics only after you give the relevant consent.
